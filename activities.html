惠斯勒 — 北美顶级山地度假胜地Whistler — North America's Premier Mountain Resort

距温哥华约2小时车程，惠斯勒是北美最知名的全季度假胜地。夏季可以体验高山徒步、山地自行车、Ziplining；冬季则是顶级滑雪胜地，学生将在专业教练指导下体验人生第一次滑雪。Just 2 hours from Vancouver, Whistler is North America's most renowned all-season resort. In summer: alpine hiking, mountain biking, ziplining. In winter: world-class skiing with professional instruction — many students' first ski experience!

维多利亚岛 — 英伦风情小城Victoria Island — Charming British Heritage City

乘坐渡轮穿越乔治亚海峡，抵达BC省省会维多利亚市。这座保留着浓厚英伦风情的小城，以精致的花园、维多利亚式建筑和高档下午茶闻名。必去：布查特花园（世界著名花卉园）、省议会大厦、贝肯山公园。Take a ferry across the Georgia Strait to Victoria, BC's capital. This city preserves a strong British heritage, renowned for its manicured gardens, Victorian architecture, and elegant afternoon tea. Must-sees: Butchart Gardens, BC Parliament Buildings, Beacon Hill Park.

UBC & SFU — 加拿大顶尖大学校园UBC & SFU — Canada's Top University Campuses

参观英属哥伦比亚大学（全球前40）和西门菲莎大学，了解加拿大高等教育体系，感受真实的大学校园氛围。专属讲解员带领参观各院系、图书馆、研究中心，让学生对未来求学方向有更清晰的认知。长期项目学生还可与招生官面对面交流。Visit UBC (global top 40) and Simon Fraser University to understand Canada's higher education system and experience authentic university campus life. Expert guides lead tours of faculties, libraries, and research centers, helping students clarify their future academic direction. Long-term students can meet admissions officers directly.

自然探险Nature Adventure

卡皮拉诺吊桥 — 雨林上的天空之桥Capilano Suspension Bridge — Bridge in the Rainforest Sky

温哥华最具代表性的自然景点之一：横跨70米高空的卡皮拉诺吊桥，以及森林树冠上的空中步道（Treetops Adventure）。配合英语讲解，了解北温哥华的温带雨林生态系统，是一次将英语学习与自然教育完美结合的体验。One of Vancouver's most iconic natural attractions: the 70m suspension bridge and the Treetops Adventure walkways high in the forest canopy. With English-language interpretation, students learn about North Vancouver's temperate rainforest ecosystem — a perfect blend of English learning and environmental education.
