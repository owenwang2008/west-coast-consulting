我们的故事Our Story

从温哥华出发，连接两个世界From Vancouver, Bridging Two Worlds

西岸留学咨询由一群热爱教育、深爱温哥华的中加双文化背景人士共同创立。我们中有在加拿大接受完整教育的华裔第二代，也有曾经历过游学体验、后来定居温哥华的"过来人"。我们深知，一次真正有质量的游学，可以改变一个孩子的人生视野。West Coast Consulting was founded by a group of passionate educators with Chinese-Canadian bicultural backgrounds who love both education and Vancouver. Some of us are second-generation Chinese-Canadians; others are former study-abroad students who settled here. We know firsthand that a truly quality study tour can change a child's entire worldview.

十年来，我们服务了超过1200位来自中国各地的中小学生，从广州、上海、北京、成都，到更多中小城市的家庭。每一个孩子回国后的成长变化，都让我们更加坚定地走在这条路上。Over 10 years, we've served over 1,200 K-12 students from across China — from Guangzhou, Shanghai, Beijing, and Chengdu to families in smaller cities. Every child's transformation upon returning home strengthens our conviction to continue this work.
