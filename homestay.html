不只是住宿，更是第二个家。我们严格筛选的本地家庭，让孩子在温暖中成长、在英语中沉浸。More than accommodation — a second home. Our carefully vetted families help students grow with warmth and immerse in English.

为什么寄宿家庭如此重要Why Homestay Matters

语言进步最快的地方，是餐桌The Best Place to Learn English Is the Dinner Table

研究表明，住在寄宿家庭的国际学生英语进步速度比住宿舍的学生快40%以上。原因很简单：在家庭环境中，英语不再是"学习的语言"，而是生活的语言——购物、聊天、看电视、做作业，无时无刻不在用英语。Research shows that international students in homestay improve their English over 40% faster than those in dormitories. The reason is simple: in a family setting, English isn't a "study language" — it's the language of life: shopping, chatting, watching TV, doing homework.

更重要的是，寄宿家庭体验让孩子在安全的环境中学会独立——独立管理时间、独立解决生活问题、独立与不同文化背景的人建立关系。这些能力，是任何课堂都无法完全给予的。More importantly, homestay helps children learn independence in a safe environment — managing their time, solving everyday problems, and building relationships across cultures. These skills cannot be fully taught in any classroom.

1

公开招募 & 初步筛选Open Recruitment & Initial Screening

通过本地社区网络和教会招募志愿寄宿家庭，提交完整家庭资料和动机说明。We recruit host families through local community networks and churches, requiring a full family profile and motivation statement.

2

背景调查 & 面试Background Checks & Interview

所有家庭成员进行BC省犯罪记录查询，并接受我们团队的视频和实地面试。All household members undergo BC Criminal Record Checks and face video and in-person interviews with our team.

3

实地家访 & 培训Home Visit & Training

协调员实地走访，检查住宿条件（独立房间、采光、取暖等），并为家庭提供跨文化接待培训。Our coordinator visits homes to inspect accommodation standards (private room, lighting, heating, etc.) and provides cross-cultural hosting training.

4

精准匹配 & 入住Smart Matching & Move-In

根据学生年龄、性格、兴趣爱好和地理位置，为每位学生精准匹配最合适的家庭。We match each student to the most suitable family based on age, personality, interests, and location proximity to school.

5

持续回访 & 评估Ongoing Follow-Up & Assessment

每两周进行一次家庭回访，收集学生和家庭双方反馈，确保住宿质量始终如一。We conduct family check-ins every two weeks, collecting feedback from both students and families to maintain consistent quality.
