🛡️ 安全与保障Safety & Protection

孩子第一次出国，我非常担心安全问题，你们如何保障？My child is traveling abroad for the first time. How do you ensure safety?▾
我们有完整的安全保障体系：①每日签到制度（早晚各一次，通过微信群同步给家长）；②中英双语辅导员全程陪伴（每8名学生配备1名辅导员）；③24/7中文紧急热线，承诺30分钟内响应；④学生专属综合保险（医疗、意外、行李全覆盖）；⑤所有工作人员持有BC省犯罪记录查询证明；⑥行程全程在温哥华本地进行，无长途跨省转移。We have a comprehensive safety system: ① Daily check-in (morning and evening, reported to parents via WeChat); ② Bilingual counselors throughout (1 per 8 students); ③ 24/7 Chinese-language emergency hotline with 30-minute response guarantee; ④ Student comprehensive insurance (medical, accident, and baggage); ⑤ All staff have current BC Criminal Record Checks; ⑥ All activities are local in Vancouver — no long inter-provincial transfers.
如果孩子生病了怎么办？What happens if my child gets sick?▾
我们为每位学生购买了涵盖急诊和门诊的综合医疗保险。一旦学生不适，辅导员会第一时间陪同前往附近的诊所或医院，并全程中文翻译协助。同时立即通知家长，并发送就诊报告。对于轻微不适，寄宿家庭也会提供基本的家庭护理。Every student has comprehensive medical insurance covering emergency and outpatient care. If a student feels unwell, a counselor will immediately accompany them to a nearby clinic or hospital with full Chinese translation assistance, while notifying parents and sending a visit report. For minor ailments, the host family provides basic home care.
家长可以随时联系到孩子吗？Can parents contact their child at any time?▾
可以。学生会使用自己的手机，家长可以随时通过微信、电话联系孩子。我们还会建立"家长+辅导员"微信群，每天分享当天的活动照片和学生状态报告，让家长实时了解孩子的情况。寄宿家庭也会直接在群里与家长互动。Yes. Students use their own phones, and parents can contact them anytime via WeChat or phone. We also create a "Parent + Counselor" WeChat group with daily photo updates and student status reports. Host families also interact directly with parents in the group.

📋 签证与手续Visa & Documentation

孩子需要办理加拿大签证吗？流程是怎样的？Does my child need a Canadian visa? What's the process?▾
是的，中国公民前往加拿大需要办理临时居民签证（TRV）或电子旅行授权（eTA）。短期和中期游学通常申请旅游签证，长期学期项目需要申请学生签证（Study Permit）。我们可以协助您准备签证材料（需额外支付服务费），提供邀请函和在读证明等所需文件。签证办理时间建议提前2-3个月开始。Yes. Chinese citizens need a Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) or eTA to visit Canada. Short and medium programs typically use a tourist visa; the long-term semester program requires a Study Permit. We can assist with visa document preparation (additional service fee applies), providing invitation letters and enrollment certificates. We recommend starting the visa process 2-3 months in advance.
孩子可以独自乘飞机来温哥华吗？Can my child fly to Vancouver alone?▾
可以。12岁以上的学生通常可以作为无陪伴未成年人（UM）乘坐国际航班，大多数航空公司提供专属无陪伴未成年人服务。我们会在温哥华国际机场（YVR）安排专人接机，确保学生安全抵达寄宿家庭。对于12岁以下的学生，建议家长陪同前往或选择有家长随行的团体出发方式。Yes. Students aged 12+ can typically fly as Unaccompanied Minors (UM) on international flights — most airlines offer dedicated UM services. We arrange a dedicated meet-and-greet at Vancouver International Airport (YVR) and safe transfer to the host family. For children under 12, we recommend a parent accompany them or choose a group departure with chaperones.

💰 费用说明Cost & Payment

费用里包含什么？有没有隐藏消费？What's included in the fees? Are there hidden costs?▾
我们承诺费用完全透明，无隐藏消费。项目费用包含：住宿（寄宿家庭含三餐）、学校课程费、全部活动和旅行费、英语课程费、保险、当地交通、机场接送、辅导员费用、结营证书。不包含：国际机票、签证费、个人零花钱。我们会在合同中列明所有费用明细，签约前请仔细阅读。We guarantee complete price transparency — no hidden costs. Included: accommodation (homestay with 3 meals), school tuition, all activities and excursions, English courses, insurance, local transportation, airport transfers, counselor fees, and completion certificate. Not included: international airfare, visa fees, and personal spending money. All cost details are listed in the contract — please read carefully before signing.
如何付款？可以分期吗？退款政策是什么？How do I pay? Can I pay in installments? What's the refund policy?▾
付款方式：支持银行转账（人民币转加币）、微信支付、支付宝。付款安排：报名时缴纳30%定金，出发前60天缴清尾款。退款政策：出发前90天以上取消，全额退款（扣除$200行政费）；60-89天取消，退70%；30-59天取消，退40%；30天内取消，不予退款。如遇签证被拒，全额退款。特殊情况（医疗原因等）可商议。Payment: bank transfer (RMB to CAD), WeChat Pay, or Alipay. Schedule: 30% deposit upon enrollment, full balance 60 days before departure. Refund policy: 90+ days before: full refund minus $200 admin fee; 60-89 days: 70% refund; 30-59 days: 40% refund; under 30 days: no refund. Visa refusals receive a full refund. Special circumstances (medical, etc.) negotiated case by case.
孩子需要带多少零花钱？How much spending money should my child bring?▾
短期（14天）建议携带CAD $300-500；中期（4-6周）建议CAD $600-1000；长期（1学期）建议CAD $1500-2500。零花钱主要用于：个人购物纪念品、额外零食饮料、个人护理用品、偶尔的额外外出费用。所有主要活动和餐饮均已包含在项目费用中。建议以加拿大现金或预付卡形式携带，不建议携带大量现金。Short-term (14 days): CAD $300-500; Medium (4-6 weeks): CAD $600-1,000; Long-term (1 semester): CAD $1,500-2,500. Spending money is mainly for personal souvenirs, extra snacks/drinks, personal care items, and occasional extra outings. All main activities and meals are included in program fees. We recommend a prepaid card or moderate cash rather than carrying large amounts.

🏠 寄宿家庭Homestay

寄宿家庭会说中文吗？孩子语言不通怎么办？Do host families speak Chinese? What if my child can't communicate?▾
我们的寄宿家庭以英语家庭为主，这正是游学的目的——英语沉浸式环境。但我们也有部分有华裔背景的寄宿家庭，可以在过渡期提供中文支持。对于英语基础薄弱的小学生，我们会优先匹配有一定中文能力或接待中国学生经验的家庭。辅导员也会在适应期内频繁与家庭联系，协助沟通。Our host families are primarily English-speaking — this is the purpose of the program: an English immersion environment. However, we do have some families with Chinese-Canadian backgrounds who can provide Chinese support during the transition. For younger students with limited English, we prioritize matching with families experienced in hosting Chinese students. Counselors also stay in close contact during the adjustment period to help with communication.
寄宿家庭的饮食可以照顾到中国孩子的口味吗？Can host families accommodate Chinese children's food preferences?▾
我们在匹配前会询问学生的饮食偏好和过敏情况，并提前告知寄宿家庭。大多数家庭会提供西式餐食（三明治、意大利面、沙拉、烤肉等），部分家庭会融入亚洲元素。温哥华是全球华人比例最高的城市之一，当地超市有丰富的亚洲食材，周末有时会去亚洲超市采购。请注意，游学的部分目的也是拓展孩子的饮食体验。如有严重过敏，请务必提前告知我们。We ask about dietary preferences and allergies before matching, and inform host families in advance. Most families provide Western meals (sandwiches, pasta, salads, grilled meats, etc.) with some incorporating Asian elements. Vancouver has one of the world's highest proportions of Chinese residents, with abundant Asian ingredients at local supermarkets. Note that part of the study tour experience is expanding children's food horizons. Please inform us of any severe allergies in advance.

📚 英语与学业English & Academics

孩子英语不好，适合参加游学吗？My child's English isn't great. Is a study tour suitable?▾
完全适合！事实上，英语进步最快的往往不是英语已经很好的学生，而是英语基础一般、但在完全沉浸环境中被"逼"出来的孩子。我们的项目为不同英语水平的学生设计了不同强度的支持：英语基础薄弱的学生会得到更多辅导员协助和较少独立的语言任务，随着适应加强，逐步提升挑战难度。只需能进行基本日常交流即可参加短期营。Absolutely suitable! In fact, students who improve the fastest in English are often not those who already speak well, but those with average English who are "pushed" by full immersion. Our programs have tiered support for different English levels: students with weaker English receive more counselor assistance and less independent language tasks, with challenges gradually increasing as they adapt. Basic daily communication ability is sufficient for the short-term camp.
游学期间孩子国内的课业会不会落下？Will my child fall behind on their Chinese school coursework during the program?▾
这是很多家长的顾虑。对于短期（14天）项目，通常安排在暑假或寒假，不影响国内课业。中期项目需要请假，我们建议与学校提前沟通，并可为学生出具参加正式教育项目的英文证明信。对于长期项目，学生将获得正式的加拿大学业成绩单，可与国内学校协商学分互认问题。我们也会提醒学生利用课余时间适当维持中文学习，避免完全断档。A common parental concern. For short-term (14-day) programs scheduled during school holidays, there's no impact on domestic coursework. Medium-term programs require a leave of absence — we advise communicating with the school in advance and can provide an official English enrollment letter. For long-term programs, students receive an official Canadian transcript, and credit recognition with Chinese schools can be negotiated. We also encourage students to maintain some Chinese studies during free time.

👨‍👩‍👧 家长关心For Parents

家长可以一同前往陪伴孩子吗？Can parents come along to accompany their child?▾
家长可以自行前往温哥华，但我们不建议在项目期间住在孩子附近或频繁干预——这反而会影响孩子的独立成长和适应过程。游学的价值之一，正是孩子在没有父母的环境中学会独立。如果家长来温哥华旅游，我们可以安排在项目结束后的家庭团聚活动，甚至协助安排短暂的亲子体验行程。Parents are welcome to travel to Vancouver independently, but we don't recommend staying nearby or intervening frequently during the program — this can actually hinder children's independence and adaptation. One of the key values of a study tour is learning to be independent without parents. If parents visit Vancouver, we can arrange family reunion activities after the program, and even help arrange brief parent-child excursion experiences.
孩子如果想提前回国或中途退出怎么办？What if my child wants to return home early or withdraw mid-program?▾
这种情况非常罕见，但我们有完善的处理机制。辅导员会首先与学生深入沟通，了解原因（通常是适应期的暂时性情绪波动），帮助解决具体问题。如果确实需要提前回国，我们会协助家长安排未成年人陪护乘机服务，并妥善办理交接。费用方面，按照合同退款政策处理，扣除已发生的服务费用。This is very rare, but we have a clear protocol. Counselors will first have a deep conversation with the student to understand the reasons (often temporary adjustment-period emotions) and help resolve specific issues. If early return is truly necessary, we assist parents in arranging unaccompanied minor escort service and proper handover. Fees are handled according to the contract refund policy, minus costs already incurred.
如何评估游学对孩子的实际效果？How do you measure the actual impact of the program on my child?▾
我们提供多维度的评估报告：①英语能力前后对比测试报告（口语、听力、词汇）；②寄宿家庭日常观察反馈报告；③学校出具的课堂参与评价（适用于中期和长期项目）；④辅导员的综合成长观察报告。此外，结营时我们会与每位学生进行一次总结性谈话，帮助他们提炼和内化这段体验。许多家长反映，孩子回国后的变化是多方面的：更愿意主动用英语沟通、更开放包容、更有主见和独立性。We provide multi-dimensional assessment reports: ① Before/after English ability test (speaking, listening, vocabulary); ② Host family daily observation report; ③ School classroom participation assessment (for medium and long-term programs); ④ Counselor comprehensive growth observation report. We also conduct a closing reflective conversation with each student to help them articulate and internalize the experience. Many parents report multifaceted changes: greater initiative to communicate in English, more open-mindedness, and stronger opinions and independence.
